Pasta Carbonara

This creamy dish of the pasta in a luxurious sauce of bacon, egg and chee is a easy-to-make treat. Adding lots of good, fresh black pepper will really add to the brightness of the dish\and make the other ingredients "pop."

Ingredents:

  • 1/2 pound bacon, diced
  • 1 Tablespoon garlic, miceed
  • 1 pound of pasta, cooked al dante
    • the trad. pasta is spaghetti, but linguine also works
    • dont overcook, will be mushy
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • salt and fresh black pepper, to taste

Directions:

  1. Cook Bacon in large pan
  2. Remove bacon from pan, keep rendered fat
  3. Add garlic and black pepper to the pan and cook til fragerent
  4. Add the bacon and pasta back to the pan cook for minute
  5. take pan of the heat and add eggs and cheese, stir quickly so the eggs thicken but don't scramble
